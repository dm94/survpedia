A scraper is a stone tool used for cleaning animal hides, shaping wood, or processing plant fibers.

## Manufacturing

1. **Flake production**: A large flake is struck from a stone core.
2. **Edge retouching**: One or more edges are worked to create a steep, sturdy, and sharp scraping surface.

## Importance

Essential for the production of leather clothing and shelter, which allowed humans to inhabit colder climates.
