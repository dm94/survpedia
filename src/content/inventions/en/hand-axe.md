A hand axe is a prehistoric stone tool with two faces that is the longest-used tool in human history.

## Manufacturing

1. **Core preparation**: A large piece of flint or other suitable stone is selected.
2. **Bifacial knapping**: Both sides of the stone are flaked to create a symmetrical, teardrop-shaped tool with sharp edges.

## Importance

A multi-purpose tool used for butchering animals, digging, and chopping wood.
