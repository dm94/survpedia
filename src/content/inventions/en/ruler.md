# Brief description
A straight strip of plastic, wood, metal, or other rigid material, marked at regular intervals, used to draw straight lines or measure distances.

## Use / Function
-   **Measurement**: Measuring length.
-   **Drawing**: Guiding straight lines.

## Operating principle
Direct comparison against a standard scale marked on the tool.

## How to create it
1.  **Body**: Find a straight branch or split a log. Plane it flat and straight.
2.  **Marking**: Use a known standard (e.g., thumb width, foot) to mark intervals. Carve notches.

## Materials needed
-   Wood/Bone/Metal

## Variants and improvements
-   **Tape Measure**: Flexible for measuring curves.
-   **Folding Ruler**: Compact storage.
