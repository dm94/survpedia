Oxygen is the chemical element with the symbol O and atomic number 8.

## Properties
It is a highly reactive gas and essential for respiration of most living organisms.

## Uses
- Steel production.
- Medicine (oxygen therapy).
- Rocket fuel (as oxidizer).
