Hydrogen is the chemical element with the symbol H and atomic number 1. It is the lightest element in the periodic table.

## Properties
It is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible diatomic gas at standard conditions.

## Uses
- Production of ammonia.
- Hydrogenation of fats and oils.
- Rocket fuel.
